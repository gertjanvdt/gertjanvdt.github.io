Wheel Of Success

This game makes the user guess a sentence within 5 tries. Senteces are stored in an array and when player starts the game one is selected at random. Then when player clicks a letter, that letter is compared to all in the sentence. If correct, the letter is displayed and otherwise player looses a life. The game ends when the sentence is guessed or the player has lost 5 lives.

About Me

In the past 10 years during my online e-commerce, tourism career I have always been interested in the front end technology and software development. While working in various positions I have always been working closely with developers in different diciplines and had a few (front end) developers in my social circle.

My goal is always to keep improving myself, learn new technologies and develop new skills. That is why I made the decision early in 2020 to start my road to become a front end developer. My focus is now to specialise myself in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. Also I have started helping a few start-ups with their Shopify site and develop custom changes for them. In the end my goal is to grow towards full stack development.

Although writing actual code and developing my own solutions is new to me, I have been solving logical and technical problems all throughout my career. I have a lot of experience testing, analysing and translating results into solutions with a methodical approach.
